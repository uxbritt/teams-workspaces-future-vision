Summary

Key Takeaways

Enterprise leaders use hierarchical navigation

Salesforce, Marketo, and Eloqua all implement nested folder structures to manage scale.

Simplify to: Workspaces + Folders + Tags

Folders organize, workspaces hold permissions/insights/AI, tags enable flexible reporting.

Flat models don't scale for enterprise

Mailchimp-style flat navigation fails beyond 50 business units — not suitable for our target market.

Clear separation of concerns

Folders for organization, workspaces for permissions/insights/AI, tags for flexible reporting.

AI capabilities are an emerging differentiator

Braze sets the bar with predictive, generative, and autonomous AI — consider roadmap alignment.
