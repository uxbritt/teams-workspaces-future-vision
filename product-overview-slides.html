Teams Plan — FML Market

4 Strategic Pillars

Based on competitive analysis and market research findings

Priority 1

Hierarchical Navigation

Parent → Region → Location tree structure that scales to 500+ business units without breaking

Workspace folders Fast context switching Tree navigation

Market finding: Flat models break at ~50 units. All enterprise leaders use hierarchical.

Priority 2

Roll-up Reporting

Aggregate metrics across all locations without switching. Identify top and struggling performers.

Org-level dashboards Performance leaderboards Cross-workspace views

Market finding: FranConnect, ServiceTitan, Crunchtime all provide real-time rollup as core value.

Priority 3

Brand & Access Control

Role-based permissions, brand-approved asset distribution, and approval workflows for campaign compliance.

Brand kit sharing Campaign approval Template locking

Market finding: Ansira + BrandMuscle lead with local co-op spend and brand-approved templates.

Priority 4

AI Capabilities

Predictive insights, generative content, and autonomous optimization. Emerging differentiator for enterprise.

Predictive analytics Content generation Smart recommendations

Market finding: Braze and SOCi set the enterprise AI bar. 150k+ AI agents deployed by SOCi.

Teams plan is the product tier for FML market

These 4 pillars differentiate from flat multi-account offerings like Mailchimp & ActiveCampaign
