Key Recommendation

Flat vs. Hierarchical

Two distinct navigation approaches

Flat

Simple dropdown, 1–2 clicks

Breaks at ~50 business units

Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign

Recommended

Hierarchical

Parent → Region → Account tree

Scales to 500+ with roll-up

Salesforce MC, Marketo, Eloqua

Recommendation: Hierarchical navigation is required for enterprise scale
