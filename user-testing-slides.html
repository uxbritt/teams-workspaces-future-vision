Summary

Key Recommendations

Based on 7 moderated + 12 unmoderated sessions

1

Replace "Folder" with "Region"

Users suggested "Region" or "Location" — terms that map to real-world geography they already understand.

2

Add Onboarding Tooltips

Users asked for guided walkthroughs and contextual hints. Design feels "flat" without guidance.

3

Clarify Hierarchy Levels

"Organization" confused users. Make the Org, Region, Workspace levels visually distinct and labeled.

Friction

Critical Pain Points

Consistent issues across novice users in both V1 and V2

01

"Mid-level folder/hub" Undefined

100% of users confused by this term. No mental model for hierarchy depth.

02

Org vs Folder vs Workspace Unclear

Users couldn't distinguish levels. "I don't know what you mean by viewing the organization."

03

Confidence Erosion

One user dropped to 1.5/5 confidence, said "I feel pretty dumb" despite navigating correctly.

04

Design Feels "Flat"

"No tips and tricks, no guidance. If I worked on it for the first time, I'd be like, what the hell?"
